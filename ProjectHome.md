<p><a href='http://code.google.com/p/marinemap'>MarineMap</a>, a web-based <a href='http://marinemap.org/decision-support-tool'>decision support tool</a> for spatial planning, is being utilized in ongoing marine planning processes in Oregon.  Goal 19 of Oregon's Statewide Planning requires the state "to conserve marine resources and ecological functions for the purpose of providing long-term ecological, economic, and social value and benefits to future generations." Oregon is developing a comprehensive <a href='http://www.oregon.gov/LCD/OCMP/Ocean_TSP.shtml'>Territorial Sea Plan</a> to guide uses of the nearshore marine environment, including the potential siting of renewable ocean energy projects.</p>
<p><a href='http://code.google.com/p/marinemap'>MarineMap</a> offers a simple, flexible and powerful means of gathering expertise from resource managers, scientists, stakeholders and public in a process of collaborative decision making.</p>
